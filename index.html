Call for Papers

Following the success of the first ISDS 2023 organized at Can Tho University (CTU), the second ISDS 2024 organized at Nha Trang University, and the third ISDS 2025 at CTU, ISDS 2026 will be held at Yuan Ze University, Taiwan. The objective is to attract domestic and foreign researchers to present outstanding and recent research in ICT. The conference supports scientific exchange and collaboration, and provides a platform for students to report and learn new results.

Topics include (but are not limited to):

  • Track 1: Intelligent Systems and Recommender Systems
  • Track 2: Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Track 3: Image Processing and Pattern Recognition
  • Track 4: Natural Language Processing

Important Dates

  • Deadline for submission: 30-July-2026
  • Acceptance notification: 07-Sept-2026
  • Deadline for final papers: 14-Sept-2026
  • Conference dates: 14-Nov-2026 - 15-Nov-2026

Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted elsewhere. Submissions must be in English and in PDF using LNCS/CCIS one-column page format. Paper length: 12-15 pages (long papers) and 6-8 pages (short papers).

Authors are encouraged to use the LaTeX template rather than the Word template. If two papers have similar quality, preference is given to LaTeX-prepared papers.

EasyChair submission URL: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=isds2026

Publications

All accepted papers will be published through one of the following methods (based on review results):

  • Top 10% papers: Journal on Information Technologies and Communications (ICT Research, ISSN 1859-3534). Fee: 150 USD (Vietnamese/Taiwanese), 200 USD (foreign authors).
  • 35% papers: Springer CCIS (Scopus). Fee: 250 USD (Vietnamese/Taiwanese), 300 USD (foreign authors).
  • 15% papers: CTU Journal of Innovation and Sustainable Development. Fee: 100 USD (Vietnamese/Taiwanese), 150 USD (foreign authors).

Selected papers, after further revision and extension (at least 30%), will be considered for special issues of Springer Nature Computer Science (SNCS), indexed in Scopus, ACM Digital Library, DBLP, Google Scholar, etc.
